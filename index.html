About

Dr. Doug Chung is a board-certified specialist in Otolaryngology-Head & Neck Surgery. He helps children and adults with problems in the head and neck area, especially conditions involving the sinuses, ear, nose and throat. Learn More

With two locations in the Braselton - Hoschton and Winder - Bethlehem areas of northeastern Georgia, Dr. Chung’s clinic is easily accessible to residents of Gwinnett, Jackson, Hall and Barrow counties and cities of Winder, Bethlehem, Loganville, Braselton, Hoschton, Jefferson, Monroe, Athens, Lawrenceville, Dacula, Buford and Flowery Branch.
